Extension Setup

Get your token from canvas-ai.herokuapp.com/settings after logging in

How to Get Your Token

  1. Visit canvas-ai.herokuapp.com
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Under "Extension Setup", copy your auth token
  5. Paste it into the field above and click "Save Token"

About

Canvas AI Helper generates AI-powered assignment drafts directly from your Canvas pages.

  • 🚀 Works on any Canvas assignment page
  • 🤖 Powered by Claude AI
  • 🔒 Your data stays private and encrypted
  • 💾 Drafts are saved to your account
