Frequently Asked Questions

Is my token secure?

Yes. Your token is encrypted at rest and only transmitted over HTTPS. It authenticates your extension with our API, similar to how an API key works. Never share your token with anyone else.

What can the extension do with my token?

Your extension can submit assignment details to get AI-generated drafts, and view/delete your saved drafts. It cannot access your Canvas account or generate new authentication tokens.

What happens if I lose my token?

You can always generate a new token here. The old token will immediately become invalid, and you'll need to update it in your extension settings.

Can I use one token for multiple browsers?

Yes, but all extensions using the same token will share your draft history. For better organization, you can generate separate tokens for different devices.

How do I revoke access?

Generate a new token to revoke the old one, or simply uninstall the extension. Your drafts will remain in your history, but the extension won't be able to access the API.
